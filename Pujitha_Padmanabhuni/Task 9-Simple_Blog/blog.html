Understanding Generative AI: Revolutionizing Creativity and Problem-Solving

Generative AI, often abbreviated as GenAI, refers to a class of artificial intelligence that can create new content—such as text, images, music, and even code—by learning from a vast amount of existing data. Unlike traditional AI models that are designed to classify or predict, generative AI models can produce novel outputs that mimic the patterns found in their training data.
